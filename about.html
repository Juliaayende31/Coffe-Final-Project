About Us
Julia's Coffee Shop

Julia's Coffee Shop started wayback in 2009 at the home grounds of the Ateneo de Manila High School Caf. Our coffee shop offers high quality food & beverages at affordable prices. Now, True Blends Tea & Coffee products are now available online. You may now enjoy your favorites at the comfort of your homes. All coffee drinks are now in liters & gallons for the family, friends and baranggay to enjoy! Now, we have True Blends Frozen Depot which offers the food our stores offer. ALL products we create are handcrafted for everyone to enjoy. You may pm us your inquiries and we will try to reply asap. For the meantime, you can browse our page to check all the products True Blends offer. Let's spread the flavor & fun in every brew!
